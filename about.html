About Dr. Shasi Vardhan Janjirala

Consultant Interventional Cardiologist, Endovascular Specialist, & Structural Heart Interventions Specialist

Pioneering advanced, minimally invasive treatments at **PACE Hospitals, Hitech City**.

Dr. Shasi Vardhan Janjirala is a distinguished Interventional Cardiologist with **over 13 years of specialized experience** in treating high-risk cardiovascular and peripheral vascular diseases. Known for his swift decision-making and meticulous surgical skills, his practice is founded on advanced techniques and unwavering patient compassion. He is committed to providing cutting-edge, patient-centered care for simple and complex cardiac and vascular procedures.

Global Certifications & Expertise

  • **Certified CTO Operator:** Expertise in specialized PCI techniques for Chronic Total Occlusions (CTO). Trained at Sakurabashi Watanabe Advanced Centre, Osaka, Japan.
  • **Certified ROTA Operator:** Skilled in Rotablation, using a diamond-tipped burr to treat severely calcified coronary artery blockages. Trained at Mount Sinai, NY, USA.
  • **Certified TAVI Operator:** Proficient in Transcatheter Aortic Valve Implantation (TAVI) and other structural interventions. Trained at UMC, Astana, KZ.

Education and Professional Milestones

  • **DM (Cardiology):** Kurnool Government Medical College
  • **Post graduation (Internal medicine):** GMCH 32, Chandigarh
  • **MBBS:** PIMS, Karimnagar

Specialized Training

  • **Pediatric Interventions:** Advanced training at Sree Chitra Tirunal, Trivandrum.
  • **Peripheral Interventions:** Specialized training at ICE Delhi.
